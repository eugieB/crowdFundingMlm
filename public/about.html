About Us

We are a savings club that helps members cultivate a savings culture for their long-term financial benefit.

Our club invests and saves money for members, sharing the interest income generated at the end of a 12-month period.

Membership is open to anyone who is committed to saving regularly, has a bank account and agrees to abide by our constitution.
